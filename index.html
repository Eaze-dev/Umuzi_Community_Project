About Us

The Community Care Brigade (CCB) is a youth volunteer group committed to local environmental protection. We offer free yard cleanup for elderly neighbors and maintain public spaces, like historic cemeteries, keeping our whole community safe and sustainable.

Read More
Our Mission

To empower youth volunteers to protect the local environment by providing free, essential cleanup and maintenance services to both elderly neighbors and historic public spaces.

Read More
Our Vision

A community where vulnerable residents and shared public lands are sustainably maintained, fostering a culture of care, respect, and environmental stewardship among young people.

What we do

What we do to protect environment

Cemetery Preservation

We clean and maintain local cemeteries, ensuring these important historical sites are preserved and treated with respect.

Elderly Yard Assistance

We assist elderly citizens in maintaining their yards and homes, providing safety, dignity, and neighborly support to those unable to do the work themselves.

Neighborhood Recycling Drives

Organizing regular collection events and educational sessions to help neighbors properly sort and reduce community waste.

Community Greening & Beautification

Planting and maintaining small community gardens, flowerbeds, and common areas to improve neighborhood aesthetics and local ecology.
